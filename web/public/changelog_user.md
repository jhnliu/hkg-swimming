# What's New

## 2026-03-21

### Improvements (latest)
- **Competitions page pagination** — Competitions now load 20 at a time with Previous/Next navigation, instead of loading all at once
- **Faster competition queries** — Optimized competition listing queries for quicker page loads

### New Features
- **My Team** — Build a personal watchlist of swimmers, no account needed. Add swimmers from their profile page, view all your tracked swimmers with their top 5 personal bests on the Team page. Save and share your team with a custom code (e.g. `/team?code=chan-family`)
- **Leaderboard: Local vs Masters filter** — Toggle between All, Local, and Masters competitions on the leaderboard
- **Leaderboard: Gender, Age Group & Season filters** — Filter rankings by gender, age group, or swimming season. Filters can be combined and are preserved when switching events
- **Appeals** — Submit data corrections or report missing results via the Appeals page
- **Compare** — Head-to-head PB comparison between any two swimmers across all shared events
- **Trends** — Biggest improvers and breakthrough qualifying swims

### Improvements
- **Faster page loads** — All pages now show loading skeletons while data loads, and club pages load significantly faster
- **Age group & season filters** now use dropdown menus instead of pill buttons for a cleaner interface
- **Swimmer profile** — PB table now shows competition placement and links to the competition page. Interactive PB progression chart shows all results and PB milestones over time
- **Club pages** — Full club names shown (English and Chinese), members by season and medals by season charts
- **Competition pages** — Per-event navigation for large competitions
- **Search** — Club names shown in full instead of codes

### Bug Fixes
- Fixed duplicate swimmers appearing on club pages
- Fixed all swimmers showing as "Women" on profile pages
- Fixed layout issues on mobile for competition pages
- Fixed display issues with certain swim times containing extra characters

### Data
- **342,327 results** across **14,897 swimmers**, **90 clubs**, and **218+ competitions** (2014–2026)
- Includes local competitions, masters, regional (18 districts), and open water events
- Swim times, splits, qualifying standards, and placements all searchable
